Communication Training

Master the art of public speaking and persuasion. Elevate your speaking skills with practical roleplaying.

About This Activity

Effective communication is the cornerstone of leadership. This rigorous training program pushes you out of your comfort zone to become a compelling and persuasive speaker.

Through practical roleplaying, impromptu speaking drills, and feedback sessions, you will overcome stage fright and learn to structure your messages for maximum impact.

What You Will Learn

  • record_voice_over Vocal Delivery: Master tone, pacing, and projection to command a room.
  • body_language Body Language: Use non-verbal cues to reinforce your message and display confidence.
  • psychology Persuasion: Structure your arguments logically and emotionally to sway your audience.
