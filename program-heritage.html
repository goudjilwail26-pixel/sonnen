Constantine Heritage Tour

Explore the historical suspension bridges and the medina with our knowledgeable guides.

About This Program

To understand where we are going, we must know where we came from. This immersive outdoor tour takes you through the rich, layered history of Constantine.

From the iconic sweeping suspension bridges spanning the Rhumel gorge to the deepest alleys of the ancient Casbah, our expert guides will bring the city's past to life.
